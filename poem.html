I met her somewhere between
a scroll and a pause…
and somehow,
she stayed longer than she should have.

I don’t know her completely—
and maybe that’s the point.

Because now I want to.
Slowly… properly…
like knowing someone,
not rushing into it.

Nerishma,

you weren’t supposed to matter this much…
but here I am,

wanting to know you more,
a little more than I should,
like I wish to understand you slowly,
through small conversations,
without forcing anything.

You were supposed to be just a pause. Somehow, you became the part I keep returning to.
