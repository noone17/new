Saju

I've known you for a year now…
and somehow, that still doesn't feel enough.

We've never met,
but you've never felt like a stranger either.

I don't want to rush this,
and I don't want to make it complicated.

I just know this—
I want to meet you,
walk with you by the beach one evening,
hold your hand…
and see where this goes.

Maybe watch a horror movie with you,
and pretend I'm not scared…
while secretly hoping you sit a little closer.

So… Saju,
will you go out with me?
